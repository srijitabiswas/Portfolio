Clarity First

I prefer interfaces that feel understood before they're learned. Every element should be easy to read and easy to act on.

Design with Intent

Every section, button and interaction should have a reason to exist. I remove anything that doesn't serve the user.

Human over Hype

I'm interested in experiences that are useful, intuitive and thoughtful — not just visually impressive.
